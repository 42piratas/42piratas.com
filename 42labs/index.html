Ânderson has spent most of a decade traveling between different countries. Parallel Echoes

PLAY

Unlike most RPGs, PARALLEL ECHOES is not a game in which you portray an avatar - a warrior, a cyborg, an alien, etc. Instead, your own self, your real you is the protagonist. Thus, by playing it, you split your timeline into two, in a manner of speaking, and find yourself managing a stormy variant of your day to day within the game.
