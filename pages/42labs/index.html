Coddiwomple

Anderson has spent most of a decade traveling between different countries. Coddiwomple, a short collection of haikulikes, his first book, is the upshot of his catharsis at the closing of those years – written while he was dealing with mal de débarquement – where he recollects random moments from some of the places he has been to during his wanderings. Lastly, unlike regular journaling games, PARALLEL ECHOES doesn't rely only on creative writing; it's also an exercise in self-analysis and a way of testing your resourcefulness in the face of uncertainty, lies, and betrayal. It's a game about calmly observing the details around you, constructing consistent rationales, and making rock-solid decisions. GAME
